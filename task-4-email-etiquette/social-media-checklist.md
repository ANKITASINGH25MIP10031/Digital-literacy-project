Do’s:
1.Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts.
2.Think before posting anything online.
3.Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely.
4.Verify information before sharing it.
5.Maintain privacy settings to protect personal data.
Don’ts:
1.Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2.Don’t spread rumors or fake news.
3.Don’t engage in cyberbullying or offensive behavior.
4.Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown attachments.
5.Don’t post inappropriate content that can harm your reputation.
