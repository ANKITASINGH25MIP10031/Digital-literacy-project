## OVERVIEW OF PROJECT
This project focuses on digital literacy and learning necessary skills to be digitally empowered. I, being a Student Digital Ambassador had to accomplish various tasks covering different areas of the digital world. These tasks included creating an infographic on awareness, building a digital portfolio, exploring coding and collaboration sites, gaining knowledge on email etiquette and an understanding of Cybercrime and Online Safety.

The purpose of these tasks was to facilitate learning with practicality and not through theorization. I was provided with various online platforms to use and digital content to create and I also learned how to work on online collaboration and communication sites as well as gained immense knowledge on safe practices over the internet.

I am confident that through this project, I have developed both technical as well as communication skills and have developed an awareness to utilize digital resources in academic as well as daily life which will be advantageous in my academic and professional future.
### MODULE SUMMARIES
#### 1. Digital literacy basics:
   This module introduced the concept of digital literacy and its importance in today’s world. It explained how students can use digital tools effectively for learning and communication. I learned about safe internet practices, useful online tools, and how to maintain a responsible digital presence. This module helped me understand that digital literacy is not just about using technology, but also about using it wisely and safely.
#### 2. Building Digital Portfolio:
   In this module, I learned how to create and manage a professional online presence. It focused on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. I understood how these platforms are used to showcase skills, connect with others, and build a digital identity. This module helped me take the first step towards creating my own portfolio, which will be useful for future internships and career opportunities.
#### 3. Coding & Collaboration Platforms:
   This module focused on learning coding through practice platforms and using collaboration tools. I explored platforms like HackerRank for coding and Google Forms for creating quizzes and collecting responses. It helped me improve my problem-solving skills and understand how digital tools can be used for teamwork and academic purposes.
#### 4. Email Etiquette & Communication:
   This module taught the importance of professional communication in the digital world. I learned how to write formal emails with proper structure and tone. It also highlighted common mistakes students make while communicating online. This module improved my writing skills and made me more aware of how to communicate clearly and respectfully.
#### 5. Cybercrime Awareness & Safety:
   This module focused on understanding different types of cybercrimes such as phishing and online fraud. I learned how these crimes happen and how to stay safe online. It also emphasized the importance of protecting personal information and being cautious while using digital platforms. This module increased my awareness about online threats and safety measures.
