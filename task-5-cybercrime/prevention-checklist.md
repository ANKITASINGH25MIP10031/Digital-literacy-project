## STAY SAFE ONLINE
1. Treat your OTP and passwords like exam secrets—never share them, no matter how urgent it sounds.
2. Before clicking any link, do a quick “Pause & Check” test: Did I expect this? Does it look genuine?
3. Use a different password for important apps (banking, email, college portals) instead of one for everything.
4. Turn on app lock or fingerprint lock for payment and social media apps.
5. Keep a habit of Googling unknown offers or job links—many scams look attractive but are fake.
6. Think before you post: “Would I be okay if this stays online forever?”
7. UPI Safety Tip 1: Remember this rule “Scan to Pay, Not to Receive.” Never scan QR codes expecting money.
8. UPI Safety Tip 2: If someone asks you to “approve a request to get money,” it’s a scam you’re actually sending money.
9. Use official apps only (Play Store/App Store) avoid downloading APK files from random links.
